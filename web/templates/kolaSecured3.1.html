Description

kolaSecured is developed with a purpose to benefit both the business and it’s customers. By getting a security rating and a hosted NFT, not only business is establishing the transparency among it’s customers but also adapting to WEB3 technology.

AI Engine:

Machine Learning Algorithms are used to perform Black-Box Testing in most non-destructive and non-intrusive way possible.

  • AI engine generate several test cases against different endpoints of your website.
Reporting and Rating Engine:

Based on result from AI Engine, a security rating and vulnerability report is generated.

  • Rating from 1 to 5 is given. 1 is least secured and 5 is most secured.
NFT Engine:

Upon rating generation, a NFT is created with the website’s favicon as object and hosted on chainlink for public validation.

  • Non-Fungible Token (NFT) works as validation of the test results of a given company.
